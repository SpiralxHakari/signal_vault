Notes

Static Halo was the debut of Xaiya Nine, recorded in fragments over three years across fractured jump sectors. It is remembered for its mix of analog pulsetronica and ritual-style requiem vocals. Several tracks show structural bleed into early Martyrcore.

Vault curators note this is the only known edition to contain the raw field-capture version of Cicada Prayer, prior to harmonization.
